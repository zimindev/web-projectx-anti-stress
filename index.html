Stress is the body's response to internal and external factors.

There is acute stress, which stimulates, and chronic stress, which harms health.

The main stress hormone is cortisol, which is produced by the adrenal glands.

"Stress is like a rubber band. It can stretch, but if it stretches too much, it will break."

To fight stress, exercise regularly and practice deep breathing to calm the nervous system.

Maintain a healthy lifestyle with good sleep, a balanced diet, and mindfulness techniques.

Social support, laughter, and positive thinking also help reduce stress and improve well-being.
