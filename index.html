What is Stress?

Stress is the body's response to internal and external factors.

There is acute stress, which stimulates, and chronic stress, which harms health.

The main stress hormone is cortisol, which is produced by the adrenal glands.

"Stress is like a rubber band. It can stretch, but if it stretches too much, it will break."

How to fight!

This page is designed to provide you with a variety of anti-stress activities and resources. Whether you're looking for games, relaxation techniques, or just some helpful tips, we've got you covered.

Remember, taking time for yourself is important!

Fight

Feeling stressed? Here are some quick tips to help you fight stress:

  • Take deep breaths
  • Go for a walk
  • Listen to music
  • Practice mindfulness
  • Talk to someone you trust

Be Strong

Remember, it's okay to feel stressed sometimes. The important thing is how you handle it. Here are some activities to help you build resilience:

  • Practice gratitude
  • Set realistic goals
  • Stay connected with friends and family
  • Engage in hobbies you love
  • Seek professional help if needed
